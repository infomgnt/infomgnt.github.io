Dorothea Strecker is a research associate in the project ‘Data practices for shaping the Open Access transformation - Analysis, recommendations, training & networking’ (OA Datenpraxis).
Following a Bachelor’s degree in Library Management at FH Potsdam, she completed the Master’s programme in Information Science at Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin in 2021.
She is an Open Science Ambassador for the Berlin University Alliance, a member of the re3data Working Group, the DataCite Metadata Working Group and the ScholCommLab.
Her main research interests are Open Science practices, in particular information infrastructures and metadata for research data.
